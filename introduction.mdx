Welcome to Volteras Connect API!

Explore the extensive reach of Volteras' API ecosystem right [here](/api-reference).
Whether you seek RESTful endpoints or wish to dive into our [WebSocketAPI](api-reference/websocket-api),
this is your launchpad.

All our authenticated endpoints uses OAuth2.

To get your access token you should have a `client_id` and a `client_secret` provided by Volteras.
